🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your College Community account directly from the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the College Community app on your device.
  2. Tap on Settings (gear icon).
  3. Navigate to Account Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm the deletion.

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

📋 What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Profile information (name, bio, profile picture, university affiliation)
  • Posts and replies you have created
  • Messages and chat history
  • All other personal data associated with your account

Note: Some anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you may be retained for analytics and service improvement purposes. For full details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

✉️ Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please include the email address associated with your account so we can verify your identity and process the request.

Email: support@collegecommunity.app

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it.
