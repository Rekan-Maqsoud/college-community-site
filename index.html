Features

Everything You Need on Campus

Built specifically for the college experience, with features that make campus life better.

Verified Students Only

Connect with real students from your university. Every user is verified with their educational email address.

Department Feeds

Browse posts from your department, college, or university. Share questions, discussions, notes, and announcements.

Group & Private Chats

Auto-created groups for your class and department. Plus, create custom groups or chat privately with classmates.

Q&A Forum

Ask questions and get answers from classmates. Upvote helpful replies and mark accepted answers.

Real-time Notifications

Get instant push notifications for likes, replies, mentions, and new messages. Stay connected with your campus.

Private & Secure

Block unwanted users, control who sees your social links, and report inappropriate content. Your privacy matters.
